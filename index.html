Surgeries

Surgery Information

Alex hold regular surgeries in the constituency of Cardiff West. These are held for local residents only and normally take place on Friday or Saturday mornings between the hours of 10am and 12.00pm. Surgeries are conducted on an appointment only basis and to attend you will need to book a time slot by contacting the MP's constituency office.
